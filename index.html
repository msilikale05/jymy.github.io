Home

Welcome to our website. JYMY is an NGO that has the Swahili meaning "Jamii Yetu, Maendeleo Yetu" and means Our Community, Our Success. In this idea, we imagine that the community in which we live is where we need to act on different issues because the more these communities develop, we also love our community and live comfortably. JYMY has been established to help the community as the way to pay, helping them with environmental issues, solid waste and other issues where immediate action is needed. We are based in Valdivia at the moment but we hope to expand more in the near future according to the needs.

About

Welcome to our website. The JYMY page shows who we are and what we do. In short, we are the group of sympathizers from different countries in Africa and Chile who have come together to help the community for their own development.Through this initiative, we believe that we are paying the community that at this moment needs help and needs someone to look at.

Please join us to create a good environment for the community that reduces many problems of them, therefore, can live clean happy and can be resistant
